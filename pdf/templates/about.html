About


Who is it for?

This is a user-friendly and custom-made digital solution for Caritas Anchor House (CAH) residents to create a strong foundational CV efficiently, but this is also a universal product in aiding employability for everyone.

Through automation and industry research, we’ve created this tool with the aim of saving resources whilst also ensuring high standards of each CV.


What templates are available?

This CV generator caters for the 3 most common target industries among residents at CAH:

  1. Customer Service
  2. Construction
  3. Security

Each template includes a single form with customizable selections for unique and tailored content using drop-down options.


How does it work?

Please view the following short 50 second video for a very quick introduction.

Steps:

  1. Select a template
  2. Complete the form
  3. Submit the form
  4. Save the PDF to your computer
  5. Use a PDF to Word converter (.pdf to .docx) and edit in Microsoft Word

Is my data private?

From our risk assessment, we considered a range of implications on data protection and privacy.

No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is asked for on this website, and so we will not ask you to provide any of the following information:

  • Full name
  • Address
  • Contact details (phone number, email address etc)
  • Other PII (Social Security number, driver’s license number, bank account number, passport number etc)

You can open and edit your CV to include your full name, address and contact details in Microsoft Word once you’ve converted the PDF file (to .docx format).

For more details about data privacy, please read our Privacy Policy and Cookies Policy.
